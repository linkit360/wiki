### Ports

* 50313 (tcp)

### Queues

AD Report

* **ReportDate** - (unix timestamp) report time
* **Campaign** - (int) campaign ID
* **TotalLpHits** - (int) All users which came on LP
* **TotalLpMsisdnHits** - (int) All users which came with MSISDN
* **TotalMo** - (int) All the valid purchase transactions
* **TotalMoSuccess**- (int) All the success purchase transactions
* **Pixels** - (int) Total count of pixels has been sent
