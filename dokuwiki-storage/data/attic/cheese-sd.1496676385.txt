### Cheese Services Description

####About service

* Subscription periodic service.
* All charging and renewal cycles processed on Cheese provider side.

####Service flow on Vostok platform

* 1. User hit LP
* 2. Vostok redirects user to the Telco confirmation page. (AK on/off)
* 3. User subscribed.
* 4. Cheese sends callback to Vostok on the subscription MO fact (if user subscribed)
